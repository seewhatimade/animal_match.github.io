# Animal Sound Match

Listen to a real animal sound and find the animal that makes it!

https://seewhatimade.github.io/animal_match.github.io/

## How to Play

1. Four animal cards appear on screen.
2. A **real animal sound** plays automatically.
3. **Tap the animal** you think makes that sound.
4. Got it right? Confetti, a chime, and your star streak goes up!
5. Got it wrong? The card shakes -- try again!
6. Press the orange **"Play Sound"** button to hear the sound again.

## Controls

- **Tap / Click** -- select an animal card
- **Play Sound button** -- replay the current sound

## Details

- 12 animals with real recorded sounds
- Score and streak counter displayed on screen
- Keep your streak going for bonus stars
